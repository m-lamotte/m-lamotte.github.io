Greetings. This is my new startpage. The idea here is to take a bunch of quotes out of a self-made 'quotes' API to keep me motivated when I open up the browser, in addition to providing a handful of useful links/references in card format.

GitHub

Repositories, style guides, notes, tips, tricks, and generally the open universe of all things discrete in natural language.
Reddit

Stay away from the nonsense and take time to appreciate or participate in good debates, or otherwise use it for insight into your passions or trades.
LinkedIn

I think it's a great application. Very useful for getting a sense of the job market and for a high-level view of what's happening in the business world.
Udemy

Fantastic resource for learning. I wish that more people I knew personally were using Udemy or any of the many other great learning platforms available.
SoundCloud

Since I have grown weary with much of what is released these days, I give most of my attention to the excellent electronic artists creating and sharing on this platform.
Spotify

Sometimes it feels like it's the only game in town, but nonetheless, it is undoubtedly the best web application for music, and its excellence is inescapable.
YouTube

The greatest web application of all time, I would have to say. An incredible universe for learning, sharing, and creating.
Globe and Mail

Canada's paper of record, it offers a fairly balanced look into a variety of affairs: the economy, business, tech, diplomacy, climate, etc.
